About this project..

In this era of hyper connectivity, where everything is linked through the internet, we enjoy convenience but also face heightened potential risks due to the accumulation of personal information. However, people often forget that the data from their simple touches or clicks, can be unknowingly leaked to others. This project aims to prompt reconsideration of these potential (and perhaps already ongoing) risks as a warning.
